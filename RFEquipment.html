My RF equipment

Here are some of the radio equipment I have built and used.

Pixie QRP CW Kit

This bad boy right here is a stronk transceiver operating on the 40m band (currently). With its 12V 2A input and a good antenna (i used a longwire), you can reach an output of almost 1.2Watts, which under perfect conditions will get you a range of about 1500KM.

The ATS-20+ V2 Radio

The ATS-20+ V2 Radio is based on a CH34x chip, similar to Arduino boards. This cute black box can operate on a frequency range of 150kHz to 30MHz on HF, and 67MHz to 108MHz on VHF.
